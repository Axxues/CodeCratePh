Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection

CodeCrate PH collects data strictly in accordance with RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012). We collect your name, email address, and payment reference numbers strictly for transaction verification.

2. Data Security

We do not store credit card details. All payments are processed via secured third-party gateways (Xendit/PayMongo).

3. Seller Anonymity

Sellers may use a pseudonym on the public marketplace. However, real identities must be submitted to the Admin team for Know Your Customer (KYC) compliance before payouts are released.
